'Buongiorno!'

"In the heart of Little Italy, nestled among the winding streets of Co. Tipperary, you'll find a taste of the Mediterranean on Saracens Street. DeAngelo's, our family-owned restaurant, has been serving the community since the 1980s, offering a unique blend of Italian tradition and Irish hospitality.

The story of DeAngelo's began over three decades ago, when our family, inspired by our rich Italian heritage and love for fine food, decided to bring a piece of Italy to Ireland. Our grandparents, rooted in the culinary arts and passionate about authentic Italian cuisine, founded DeAngelo's with a simple philosophy - to serve food that speaks to the heart, crafted with love and authenticity.

5/6 Saracens Street, Little Italy.
